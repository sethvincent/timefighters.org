Adventures in Creative Living, Pt 1
March 27, 2009 - 9:44pm narrative Lavore

It's 1:00 am on Sunday, December 28, and I am flipping through a recent impulse-buy, a book called Dwelling Portably. It''s a compilation of newsletters about portable dwelling and long camping. I'm looking to see if they have any alternatives to refrigeraters. I haven't found any yet. I'm trying to decide if I could do this. My alarm is set for 8:00 am and in the morning (the real morning), when I will put my left and right feet into two pairs of socks and big rain boots and muck my way downtown to the transit center, where I will wait for the bus.The bus will take me to look at look at a 12x12 cabin with loft. It has no utillities, only a wood-stove for heat and solar powered light. Which might be a little extreme right now, in the middle of winter. But if not now, then when?
