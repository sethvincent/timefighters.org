Free Meals from Olympia Food Not Bombs
November 6, 2008 - 1:26am micro-news sabalu

Olympia Food Not Bombs shares free meals every Saturday at 4:30 p.m. outside of the Olympia Timberland Library (8th Ave. and Franklin St.). Food Not Bombs will contnue to share meals in its normal location during library renovations. For more information, please write to olympiafnb (at) gmail (dot) com.
